# Competitive Analysis

## Analysis Framework

### Identify Competitors

#### Direct Competitors
- Same product category
- Same target audience

#### Indirect Competitors
- Different product
- Same need fulfillment

#### Potential Competitors
- Adjacent markets
- New entrants

## Dimensions to Analyze

### Product
- Features comparison
- Pricing model
- Technology stack

### Market Position
- Target audience
- Brand perception
- Market share

### Strengths
- What do they do well?
- Unique advantages?
- Customer loyalty?

### Weaknesses
- What are they missing?
- Customer complaints?
- Market gaps?

## Tools
- Product hunt
- SimilarWeb
- G2/Capterra
- App stores
- Social media
