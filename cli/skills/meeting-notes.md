# Meeting Notes

## Before Meeting

### Confirm Agenda
- Send topics in advance
- Set time expectations
- Assign note-taker

### Prepare Template
- Date and attendees
- Agenda items
- Action items from last meeting

## During Meeting

### Who, What, When
- Who said what
- Key decisions
- Action items with owners

### Categories

#### Decisions Made
- What was decided
- Rationale briefly

#### Action Items
- What needs to be done
- Who owns it
- Due date

#### Questions Raised
- Items needing follow-up
- Who will answer

#### Next Steps
- Next meeting date
- Deliverables

## Note-Taking Tips

### Active Listening
- Don't write everything
- Focus on key points

### Abbreviations
- Use abbreviations
- Create your own system

### Quick Capture
- Quote key phrases
- Note URLs immediately

## After Meeting

### Send Within 24 Hours
- To all attendees
- CC stakeholders

### Structure
1. Date and attendees
2. Key decisions
3. Action items (table)
4. Next steps

### Example Template
```
MEETING NOTES: [Title]
Date: [Date]
Attendees: [Names]

DECISIONS
- [Decision 1]
- [Decision 2]

ACTION ITEMS
| Action | Owner | Due |
|--------|-------|-----|
| [Task] | [Name] | [Date] |
| [Task] | [Name] | [Date] |

NEXT MEETING
- Date: [Date]
- Topics: [Topics]
```
