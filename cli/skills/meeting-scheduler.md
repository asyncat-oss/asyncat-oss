# Meeting Scheduling

## Before Scheduling

### Confirm Need
- Could this be an email?
- 1:1 or group?
- What's the goal?

### Prepare Agenda
- Topics to cover
- Time needed per topic
- Desired outcome

## Best Practices

### Duration
- 25 or 50 min, not 30 or 60
- Shorter is usually better
- Default to 25 min

### Timing
- Mid-morning (10-11)
- After lunch (1:30-3)
- Avoid ends of day

### Invites
- Required only
- Optional attendees noted
- Include agenda

## Agenda Template

```
MEETING: [Title]
DATE: [Date]
TIME: [Duration]
ATTENDEES: [Names]

AGENDA
1. [Topic] - [Time]
2. [Topic] - [Time]

PREP
- [Person]: [Task]
- [Person]: [Task]

OUTCOME
- [What we'll decide/have]
```

## RSVP Strategy
- Accept: Will attend
- Decline: Can't make it
- Tentative: May join

## Follow Up
- Send notes within 24h
- Include action items
- Schedule next if needed
