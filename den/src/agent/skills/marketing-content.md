# Marketing Content

## Copywriting Frameworks

### AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)
- **Attention**: Hook with a bold claim, question, or pain point
- **Interest**: Explain the problem, show understanding
- **Desire**: Present the solution, benefits, social proof
- **Action**: Clear CTA with urgency or incentive

### PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solve)
- **Problem**: Name the pain point your audience feels
- **Agitate**: Make it worse — show consequences of inaction
- **Solve**: Present your solution as the way out

### FAB (Features → Advantages → Benefits)
- **Feature**: What the product does
- **Advantage**: How it's different/better
- **Benefit**: What the user gets (emotional outcome)

### Before-After-Bridge
- **Before**: The current painful state
- **After**: The ideal outcome
- **Bridge**: How your product gets them there

## SEO Content

### On-Page SEO Checklist
- Target 1 primary keyword per page
- Title tag: 50-60 chars, keyword early
- Meta description: 150-160 chars, compelling + keyword
- H1: 1 per page, includes keyword
- URL: short, hyphenated, keyword-rich
- Internal links: 2-5 per page
- Image alt text with keywords
- Content: 1500+ words for competitive terms

### Keyword Strategy
- Head terms: 1-2 words, high volume, high competition
- Long-tail: 3+ words, lower volume, higher conversion
- Use `web_search` and `search_images` for keyword research

## Social Media

### Platform Guidelines

| Platform | Max Length | Tone | Best Time |
|----------|-----------|------|-----------|
| Twitter/X | 280 chars | Witty, timely | 8-10am, 12pm |
| LinkedIn | 3000 chars | Professional | Tue-Thu 7-8am |
| Instagram | 2200 chars | Visual, aspirational | 11am-1pm |
| Facebook | 250 chars optimal | Conversational | 1-4pm weekdays |

### Post Structure
1. Hook (first line = the headline)
2. Value (tip, insight, story)
3. CTA (link, comment, share)

### Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 per post (Instagram: up to 30 but 5-10 optimal)
- Mix: 1 branded + 2-3 niche + 1 trending
- Research with `web_search`

## Email Campaigns

### Subject Line Rules
- 6-10 words, under 50 chars for mobile
- Avoid ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation
- Personalization: +26% open rate
- A/B test subject lines always
- Preview text: 40-90 chars that complement the subject

### Email Structure
1. **Subject + Preview**: Hook in under 90 chars total
2. **Opening**: Personal, reference context
3. **Body**: 1 main point, scannable, benefit-focused
4. **CTA**: Single, clear, above the fold
5. **Signature**: Professional, with secondary CTA
